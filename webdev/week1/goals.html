My Current Goals

I started this almost 10 years ago. I went through a divorce, got custody of my kids, and had to drop my classes. So I have been waiting for life to calm down so I could go back and finish my degree. I was the first person in my family to drop out of high school and soon I will be the first with a college degree.

Before moving to Springfield I ran my own shop and worked crazy hours. So I sold my tools the first week. I told everyone I was going to work in IT and I would never touch a wrench again...
I have been building engines ever since and replaced/grew my tool collection.

Being self-taught and building a broad range of projects has left gaps in my knowlage and tends to make me doubt myself, this is very problematic looking for a job. Being around other like minded people and seeing the work they do will hopefully help with that.
